Rivefront Art Gallery//"The Roar Within"
Dates: March 4-April 24, 2020
Artwork on Display: Slim Thicc

Gallery Statement: “Sexism has never rendered women powerless. It has either suppressed their strength or exploited it.” -Bell Hooks During Women’s History Month we are exhibiting large works by women that dynamically showcase the power of female creative expression. We will feature works that challenge notions of what the feminine is, gender identities, definitions and the evolution of family structures.
